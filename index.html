About Me

Welcome to my academic homepage. I am a researcher and professor specializing in engineering design methodologies, modular product platform strategies, and Design for Additive Manufacturing (DfAM). My primary research objective is to advance system sustainability and lifecycle efficiency through structured modular architecture and additive manufacturing integration.

Our laboratory also focuses on Physical AI talent development frameworks, product complexity metrics for early-stage ESG integration, and autonomous drone system design architectures.
